Introduction

Across underserved communities in Nigeria, data reveals that millions of youths remain out of school, and many within the system lack structures for growth. Ignite 30 aims to combat the effects of poverty, limited exposure, and lack of mentorship by instilling hope, clarity, and tools for change.

Program Aim and Objectives

Ignite 30 is a two-hour development session designed to inspire and unlock potential in marginalized youth. It fosters personal growth, leadership, and exposure to global opportunities.

  • - Awaken purpose and growth mindset
  • - Connect to internships, scholarships, and fellowships

Date and Venue

Ebonyi State 16th May 2025
Kano State 4th June 2025
Osun State 11th June 2025

Expected Impact

  • - Renewed vision and mindset
  • - Basic goal setting and financial planning skills
  • - Exposure to leadership and global opportunities
  • - Access to mentorship and learning resources
  • - Motivated pursuit of personal/professional growth
